# DGD2003_Stanislau_Ruskikh


Name: "Blood Sun".

Genre: Horror, Top Down Shooter.

Concept: The player will be playing for some doll that dressed in school uniform. You wake up on the school floor. You didn't remember anything about who you are or where are you. Next to you stands three meters tall man covered in shreds of clothes, sunflowers are growing from his body, he is wearing golden mask of smiling Sun. He greets you with admiration and introduces himself as your teacher Mr. Fraund. Then Mr. Fraud tells you start your lesson. And after this player starts fighting against hordes of monsters.


USP: Mysterious Story, Unique Characters, PS1 Style Graphics, Dynamic Game Loop.


Art: Art Style will be inspired by PS1 games with modern approach. The goal here to make player feel uncomfortable and at the same time feel that he doesn't belong in this world.


Audio: Atmospheric and Thrilling.


UI: Health Bar, Points, Timer.




Game Loop: Player starts weak, but killing enemies player earns currency that he is using to make himself stronger. At the end of each run player fights boss.

Average Time for One Run: 10 min.

Current State of the Game: Game now in development, and at the moment not ready for a full release.
